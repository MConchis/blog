On Writing Short Essays

Nov 15, 2025 • 600 words

Short essays are exercises in reduction. They require you to choose a single idea and hold it clearly, trimming the rest.

Start with one question

When you strip away anecdotes and side-tracks, the core question remains. Aim the piece at that question and let the structure follow.

Keep reading small, and take notes — the practice compounds.
